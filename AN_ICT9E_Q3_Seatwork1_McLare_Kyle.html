The Fast Saga (F9) 2021

F9 is the ninth chapter in the saga that has endured for almost two decades

Dominic Toretto

He is an ex-convict, brave street racer, and mechanic. Dom's group has repeatedly carried out high-speed semi-hijackings throughout the series, taking millions of dollars' worth of goods. He has eluded justice for the majority of his life. He has retired and settled down with his wife, Letty, and his son, Brian.

Jakob Torreto

He is a major anti-hero in the Fast and Furious franchise, serving as a mentioned character in The Fast and the Furious and the central antagonist of F9, the ninth movie of the Fast & Furious Saga.

Letty Ortiz

She is the childhood sweetheart of Dominic Toretto, turned street racer, mechanic, and thief. When she volunteered to go undercover to expose Arturo Braga for the FBI, she was nearly killed and suffered from temporary amnesia as a result

Roman Pearce

He is the childhood friend of Brian O'Conner. A protagonist in The Fast and the Furious franchise, Roman is a former convict, arrested for harboring stolen cars, who won his freedom after taking down the drug lord Carter Verone with Brian.

Tej Parker

He is an old friend of Brian O'Conner and the best friend of Roman Pearce. He is a high-ranking member of Dominic Toretto's crew.
